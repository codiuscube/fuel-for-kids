Summer TEFA Updates
Exciting News and Important Updates
Wildcat Families,

We hope that you all are enjoying a wonderful summer!

As you all know and saw through multiple emails this spring, the state of Texas began the rollout of Texas Education Freedom Accounts to Texas families to provide more people the blessing of educational choice for their children.

Currently, we have 107 students who have received TEFA accounts for next school year!

Of that number, there are 20 new-to-NBCA families represented in that total. The majority of our recipients were current families.

For those of you that thought, "I am not wasting my time applying," we would encourage you to apply next year!

Helpful TEFA Information
23,000 of the 100,000 families (not students!) who received TEFA awards have NOT opted into the program.

That is a staggering number! If you applied, and received an award, and have not opted in, you must do so by July 15th.

There are still 12,860 students on the Tier 2 waitlist. The comptroller will release more waitlisted families within the next 2 weeks.

The state is hopeful that most of Tier 2 will be awarded - but there are no guarantees, obviously.

Here are a few other helpful reminders:

If you applied for TEFA, check your Odyssey portal.
Be sure to opt in, if you received an award.
If you are in Tier 2, check your portal often over the next 6 weeks.
(It is unlikely families in Tier 3 will be awarded.)
THANK YOU to current TEFA families for their graciousness, kindness, patience, understanding, and generally taking care of their business.
If you receive an award or if you have any questions, please contact Nanette Jones. If you would like to schedule a phone call with Nanette, you may click HERE.
This is a very exciting season for Christian education and for families that desire for their children to receive a Christ-centered education!

We are thrilled to see the fruits of this program and will continue to keep our families informed as we have more relevant information.

With gratitude and excitement,
