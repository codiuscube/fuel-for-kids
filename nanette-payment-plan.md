# Email to Nanette Jones — TEFA waitlist update + payment plan ask

## Main message

Hi Nanette,

In the spirit of transparency, we just got our approximate TEFA waitlist position — somewhere in the 30,000–50,000 range. My honest read is that the odds are not good. If a spot did open up, we likely wouldn't find out until late July or even August.

That makes the tuition obligation / withdrawal penalty after June 30 really important for us to understand. To restate the question from my last email: Michelle previously mentioned that if families let the school know by June 30 that they will not be enrolling, there is no tuition penalty. If we needed to make a final decision after June 30 because of TEFA, but before the July 6 FACTS payment, what tuition obligation or withdrawal penalty would apply?

We're also eagerly awaiting the NBCA scholarship decision. That, along with the penalty question, will help us see the full financial picture before the first FACTS payment.

With that in mind, I wanted to ask whether there's any flexibility on the FACTS payment plan to align it with the TEFA disbursement schedule. We're in a tough spot, and we would hate to have to unenroll because of a timing mismatch if there's a way to make it work.

A couple of possibilities I wanted to float:

- A plan where payments start in August with about four payments spread through the year, roughly tracking when TEFA funds arrive.
- A reimbursement arrangement where we stay on the current schedule and the school refunds us as each TEFA tranche comes through.

On the current 10-month plan, even in the best case where we get pulled off the waitlist, we'd likely be fronting tuition for months before reimbursement.

I want to be clear that we're committed to NBCA and to our kids being there. I'm just trying to find a way to make the cash flow work if TEFA does eventually come through for us, and to understand our options if it doesn't. We're looking at other ways to make this work on our end as well.

Thank you so much for thinking through this with us.
