# Email to NBCA — request to extend the penalty-free withdrawal deadline to July 31

## Main message

Hi Nanette,

I wanted to raise one specific request in the spirit of transparency, because the calendar has put us in a genuine bind.

We got our approximate TEFA waitlist position and it's in the 30,000–50,000 range — Tier 3. Honestly, the odds aren't good, and NBCA's own summer update said as much (that it's unlikely Tier 3 families will be awarded this year). We're planning as if TEFA won't come through and treating it as upside only.

Here's the timing problem: the penalty-free withdrawal deadline is June 30, but the largest TEFA waitlist event of the year — the July 15 opt-in/opt-out deadline, when the biggest wave of awarded families drops out and seats cascade down the list — falls about two weeks *after* that. So as it stands, we'd have to make a final, penalty-bearing decision on June 30 without knowing the one piece of information that could change it. After June 30 the penalty is steep (10% in July, ~$4,800; 20% in August), and we can't responsibly gamble that on a cascade that may never reach our band.

My request: would NBCA be willing to grant us a written extension of the penalty-free withdrawal date to July 31? That would let us see the July 15 cascade play out before committing, instead of deciding blind two weeks early.

I want to be clear that we're committed to NBCA — we've accepted the $12,000 scholarship and our intention is to have our kids there. This isn't about hedging away from the school; it's about not being forced into a penalty by a two-week calendar mismatch that's entirely outside our control. If you grant the extension, our plan is simple: ride out July 15, and if nothing changes, we stay and pay. If the answer is no, we understand, and we'll make our call by June 30 accordingly.

Happy to jump on a quick call if that's easier. Thank you so much for working through this with us.

Mahalo,
Cody Iddings
